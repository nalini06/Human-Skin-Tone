About The Project



Human skin color ranges from the darkest brown to the lightest hues. Differences in skin color among individuals is caused by variation in pigmentation, which is the result of genetics, the exposure to the sun, or both.
The report depicts our approach towards finding the skin tone and classifies them with some efficient libraries like django and opencv. The detailed information is available in the following sections.
